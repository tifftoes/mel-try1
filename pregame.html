Summoner Spells

The only recommended combination for Mel
🔵 Flash — Must Use Every Game

Flash is the best summoner spell in the game, hands down. Especially on mages — which have intentionally low mobility — it's essential to have an escape option. This is a MUST USE in every single game. No exceptions.

🟣 Teleport — Only Recommended Second Spell

TP is the only second summoner evandabank recommends on Mel. Almost all midlaners run it, and crucially, you are not in range to use Ignite for the majority of fights — Mel plays from too far away for short-range summoners to be reliable.

Runes

Standard rune page — Arcane Comet every game
Why Arcane Comet?

Mel's long range makes it nearly guaranteed that Q will proc Comet and Scorch on every trade — providing roughly 50 bonus damage early. This heavily outshines First Strike and Summon Aery. Comet is the only keystone Mel should ever run.

Why Scorch?

Scorch can feel like a second Keystone in lane — 20 damage every 10 seconds is enormous early on. Hold an ability to proc it on cooldown. By your first reset, Scorch may have already dealt 200+ damage. It provides nothing late game, but the early lane dominance it enables is unmatched.

Why Presence of Mind?

Mel is one of the most mana-hungry mages — Radiant Volley costs 110 mana at max rank. Presence of Mind gives back mana on procs and restores 15% max mana on takedowns. Not the strongest secondary rune, but it enables extra casts in the early game that other options can't match.

Why Legend: Haste?

Running a burn build (Blackfire Torch + Liandry's) means having abilities off cooldown to stack burns is critical. 15 ability haste from Legend: Haste is a genuine game-changer. Current mage items make it almost impossible to reach meaningful haste without this rune.

Items

Build path and situational picks
💍
Doran's Ring
Every game, no exceptions. Mel cannot make good use of other starting items.
🧪
2× Health Potions
Start your first potion at ~180–200 damage taken. Upgrade to Refillable on first or second reset.
👁️
Warding Totem
Must purchase on every champion. Swap to Farsight Alteration in the late game.
First reset priority: Spend ALL your gold — do not leave base with 300g unspent. Build toward Lost Chapter first (it builds into all mage starting items and gives mana on level-up). Only buy generic boots before completing your first full item.
👟
Sorcerer's Shoes
Default boots in all cases. Best upgrade of all shoe types — always build these if you win Feats of Strength.
👟
Ionian Boots of Lucidity
Run instead of Sorcerer's Shoes only when the enemy has 2–3 champions with significant magic resist built. Swiftness boots are never worth it — Mel doesn't need gap-closing movespeed.

This is a burn build. The goal is to apply persistent damage through Blackfire Torch and Liandry's procs, stacking burns every time abilities come off cooldown.

🔥
Blackfire Torch
Best first item. Burning effect on every ability landed. Provides the mana needed to sustain expensive spells early.
🌋
Liandry's Torment
Second item. Burn does more damage than Blackfire, but gives HP instead of mana — building it first causes OOM issues.
🎩
Rabadon's Deathcap
Third or fourth item. Multiplies total AP by 30% — massive power spike for poke and sustained burn damage.
🕳️
Void Staff
Build when 2–3 enemies have significant magic resist. Mathematically gold-efficient at ~120 MR on the enemy.
⌛
Zhonya's Hourglass
Third or fourth vs burst/engage comps, AD assassins (Zed, Talon), or Malphite. Stasis is the best active in the game.
🔮
Banshee's Veil
Fourth or fifth. Underrated. Best against heavy magic damage or champions that can't poke the spellshield off (e.g. Annie).
Bloodletter's Curse

Extremely underrated. Two Mel ability casts can apply full stacks — a near-instant 30% magic shred. If you have 3 AP champions on your team, this becomes a must-buy third item. Only one champion per team should build it.

Crypt Bloom

Very situational — hard to tell when you're getting real value. Not much different from Void Staff; both are anti-MR items. Build whichever is stronger in the current meta, or just default to Void Staff (simpler).

Cosmic Drive

Gives movespeed on ability hits — a unique stat that requires skill to use properly. evandabank personally does not build this and recommends most players avoid it unless they're confident in kiting with the speed bonus.

Null-Magic Mantle (Early)

Buy this on your first reset in lanes where magic burst is a death threat — Annie, LeBlanc, Sylas. Upgrade into Verdant Barrier / Banshee's later. Never buy Mercury Treads — they are a bait on mages.

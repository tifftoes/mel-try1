Welcome & about the creator
evandabank
Grandmaster · Bot & Mid · 13 Years Playing

Hi, I'm Evandabank and welcome to my Mel guide. I've been playing LoL for 13 years, since I was 7 years old — starting in Season 3. I've successfully hit Masters on every role and am currently Grandmaster on both Bot and Mid.

Since Mel was released, she quickly became one of my favorite champions due to her long range and oppressive agency. This guide details everything for Mel — including all of her matchups. It took countless hours, and if you find it helpful, check out my other links for additional content.

💬 Feel free to PM me on Mobafire or message me on Discord. Educational content is also available on Patreon — the best way to support if you choose. Good luck out there on the Rift.

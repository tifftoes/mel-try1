Aphelios

Aphelios is high damage but immobile. Keep spacing, avoid root combos, and prioritize healing from minion wave trades.

  • 1Force him to use Phase Rush early.
  • 2Play around his weapon rotation.
  • 3Engage after he spends Crescendum.
Ashe

Ashe's utility comes from poke and ult. Dodge Volley with dash/positioning and punish her low mobility when her Hawkshot is down.

  • 1Watch for Enchanted Crystal Arrow.
  • 2Bait and sidestep her slow shot.
  • 3Roam with support after level 6 if lane freezes.
Aurelion Sol

Aurelion Sol can zone with stars but is vulnerable to jungle pressure and hard engages. Stay near minions and dodge Q burst path.

Brand

Brand trades heavily through AoE burn. Avoid standing in skillshot clusters and engage after he uses Pillar.

Caitlyn

Caitlyn wins early range trades. Respect her trap zones and look to fight where she can’t extend safely.

Corki

Corki's poke is strong; avoid wide position and punish his shorter trade windows between package usage.

Draven

Draven is fragile but snowballs. Force all-ins when axes are reset and don't clump for spinning axe swings.

Ezreal

Ezreal kites and plays safe. Walk into his Mystic Shot only after a few casts and use hard CC to lock him down.

Heimerdinger

Heimerdinger can push and siege with turrets. Clear them quickly and look for flank engages while his abilities are on cooldown.

Hwei

Hwei relies on burst and dash resets. Punish her cooldown windows and keep her from hitting melee range safely.

Jhin

Jhin has strong wave control and crit poke. Dodge Deadly Flourish and trade when he has no grenade.

Jinx

Jinx is vulnerable before reset. Force fights around her passive and avoid her Rocket-based AoE in choke points.

Kai’sa

Kai’sa scales with adaptive damage. Respect her evolving build and keep vision around dragon pit for roams.

Kalista

Kalista can kite with Rend. Avoid extended trades when she has numerous spears stacked.

Karma

Karma pokes with Q->E. Dodge Soulflare and punish when shield is down.

Karthus

Karthus has global impact but weak laning. Snowball his early levels with pressure and avoid being forced into low-health ult trades.

Kog’Maw

Kog’Maw is siege-heavy and immobile. Punish him before he builds Guinsoo and use Grievous wounds to limit on-hit sustain.

Lucian

Lucian is strong in short trades. Respect his dash combos and counter-engage after he expends Relentless Pursuit.

Lux

Lux bursts from range. Avoid binding roots and stay behind minions vs her ultimate.

Mel

Mirrored matchup: emphasize your own strengths and look for level 6 fight over objective control.

Miss Fortune

Miss Fortune bullies with Double Up. Side-step Make It Rain and punish cooldowns on Bullet Time.

Morgana

Morgana's Black Shield counters CC. Play around it and strike when it’s not available.

Nilah

Nilah excels in all-ins and mobility. Deny her ultimate stack value and avoid standing in her W blade edge.

Samira

Samira snowballs with Style points. Force her to miss W and avoid grouped dashes after level 6.

Senna

Senna is poke-heavy. Close gaps with movement boots and avoid thresh-like long-range root chains.

Seraphine

Seraphine can chain CC. Disrupt her ult and pressure in short trades before she stacks Song.

Sivir

Sivir is strong in wave clear and spell shield. Bait her E and punish when it’s on cooldown.

Smolder

Smolder is dangerous in prolonged fights. Stay spread and avoid lingering in AoE firefields.

Tristana

Tristana has kill pressure with her jump. Punish level 2 spike and ward the river for her roams.

Twitch

Twitch scales with stealth. Track his Venom and cut off his flanks before teamfights.

Varus

Varus excel in poke fractional trades. Burst his playmaker and avoid his chain of corruption corridors.

Vayne

Vayne is lethal in duels. Keep distance from her tumble and condemn positions, punish her before Blade of the Ruined King.

Xayah

Xayah uses feather traps. Play around feather placement and dive when her ultimate is down.

Yunara

Yunara controls spacing with empowered strikes. Use burst windows when her shields are consumed.

Zeri

Zeri is mobile with continuous poke. Lock her with hard CC and avoid extended skirmishes where she repositions easily.

Ziggs

Ziggs is powerful at siege and disengage. Close the distance safely and force him to use Satchel to escape.

Zyra

Zyra zone controls with plants. Clear plants quickly and take trades after she uses her root.

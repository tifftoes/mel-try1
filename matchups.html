Matchup Guide

Key strategies for top matchups
vs Ahri

Ahri is a strong matchup. Use W to reflect her Q and E. Poke with Q and stack for R.

  • 1Reflect Ahri's Q with W
  • 2Avoid her charm in lane
  • 3Outscale in mid game
vs Yasuo

Yasuo is tricky. Use W to block his tornado. Position carefully to avoid his dives.

Focus on farming and scaling. Use your range advantage.

vs Zed

Zed requires careful W usage. Reflect his shurikens. Stay at max range.

Use E to root him if he overcommits.

Mid Game & Teamfighting

Mid Game & Teamfighting

Objective rotation, teamfight positioning, and ult usage for dominant mid game performance.

Objective Control

Dragon, Herald, and tower priorities
Dragon Priority

Cloud, Infernal, and Mountain dragons are your top priorities. Ocean and Hextech are secondary. Elder is always a take if available.

  • 1Cloud Dragon: Essential for wave clear and poke
  • 2Infernal Dragon: Boosts your damage output significantly
  • 3Mountain Dragon: Provides tankiness for teamfights
Herald Control

Secure Rift Herald for Baron pressure. Use it to push towers and create space.

Herald is crucial for mid game tower pressure. Coordinate with your team to take it safely.

Teamfighting

Positioning, engagement, and ult usage
Positioning

Stay in the backline to poke and stack. Use your range advantage to control the fight.

Position behind your tanks but with clear line of sight for your abilities.

Engagement

Look for picks with E into Q. Use W to reflect key abilities.

Engage when you have W up or when the enemy is grouped.

Ultimate Usage

Save R for when enemies are in execute range. Prioritize high-priority targets.

Use R to finish off low-health enemies or to secure kills in teamfights.

Item Upgrades

When to upgrade and what to build
Upgrade Timings

Upgrade around 15-20 minutes depending on game state. Focus on AP items.

  • 1Rabadon's at 15-18 min if ahead
  • 2Void Staff against MR teams
  • 3Zhonya's for survivability
